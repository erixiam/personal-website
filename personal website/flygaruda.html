FlyGaruda App Revamp

My take on my country's flag carrier airline app: Garuda Indonesia.

BACKGROUND

Garuda Indonesia is Indonesia's airline flag carrier. They are a member of SkyTeam and the second-largest airline of Indonesia after Lion Air.

Garuda Indonesia is not to be taken light of. They have been awarded for the World's Best Cabin Crew from 2014 - 2018. They alo have a 5 star Covid-19 airline safety rating, guaranteed to make your flight hygienic and safe.

RESEARCH

Analysing reviews on the app store

I started my research on FlyGaruda's app store page. I was absolutely horrified when I saw that my country's flag carrier official app only has a 2 star rating out of 5 (3 stars in Indonesia). I then see the complaints users have about the app.
